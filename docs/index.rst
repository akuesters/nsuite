NSuite
======

NSuite is a framework for maintaining and running benchmarks and validation tests
for multi-compartment neural network simulations on HPC systems. It has been
developed as a joint collaboration between the Swiss National Supercomputing
Center (CSCS), and Forschungszentrum JÃ¼lich.

NSuite's development was fully funded by the European Union's Horizon 2020
Framework Programme for Research and Innovation under the Specific Grant
Agreement No. 785907 (Human Brain Project SGA2).
